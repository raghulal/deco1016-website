Just some terms and conditions...

  • I am an Australian citizen or a permanent resident of Australia or a temporary overseas visitor.

  • I have a NSW residential address.

  • I am 17 years of age or older.

  • I have never previously held a driver licence other than a learner licence and have successfully completed the Hazard Perception Test OR I have previously held a driver licence (other than a learner) in Australia or Overseas and am applying for the issue of a NSW driver licence.

  • If I am under 25 years of age - hold a current learner driver licence or equivalent for at least 12 months - completed 120 hours of supervised driving practice of which 20 hours being night time driving. I am 25 years of age or older - hold a current learner driver licence. I have previously held a drivers licence (other than a learner) in Australia. I hold or have previously held an overseas drivers licence (other than a learner) and am converting to a NSW licence.

  • I can provide a completed Licence Application form (RMS 1001).

  • I can provide satisfactory Proof of Identity to meet the Roads and Maritime Services requirements.

  • I can pass an eyesight test and declare that I am medically fit to hold a licence.

  • My licence is not currently suspended or ineligible, or I am eligible following such period.

  • I have no outstanding fines with Revenue NSW.

  • I have no restrictions in force for improper conduct.

  • I will bring the prescribed fee for the licence for when I pass the test.
